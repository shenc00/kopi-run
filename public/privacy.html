Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-12

Kopi Run ("the app") is a simple tool for coordinating group drink orders. This policy explains what data the app handles.

What we collect

When you create or join an order, the app stores:

  • The order name and share code
  • Names you enter for yourself or others in the order
  • Drink selections and notes you enter

No account, login, email address, or phone number is required or collected. No advertising or analytics tracking is used.

How data is used

Data is used only to display and coordinate the group order in real time between people who have the order's share link. It is not sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose beyond running the app.

Storage

Order data is stored in a Supabase database. Anyone with a given order's share link/code can view and add to that order, by design — this is how the app works without accounts. Do not share order links with people you don't want seeing or adding to that order.

Data retention / deletion

Orders are not automatically deleted. To request removal of specific data, contact us using the details below.
